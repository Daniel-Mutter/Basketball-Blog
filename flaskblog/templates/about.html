A Quickstart Guide:

BasketballMeetup.com makes finding pick-up basketball games easy! With our FREE, simple website you'll find yourself tearing it up on the court in no time! To find or create pick-up games:

  1. Make an account by clicking on the "Register" button in the top right
  2. Create an account using a unique username, email, and password
  3. Edit your account by clicking on "Account" and customize your profile Picture
  4. Click on "Create Game" and specify the location, time, and capacity (e.g. 5v5)
  5. Click on the post to either update (odd number of people and need 1 more) or delete (you just realized you have a test tomorrow)
